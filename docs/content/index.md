# Welcome to PhoenixCore Documentation!

**PhoenixCore** is a GregTech Modern focused coremod for [Phoenix Forge Technologies](https://github.com/Phoenixvine32908/Phoenix-Forge-Technologies) designed to supplment the kubejs recipes, and to break the limits on what we think is possible.

!!! danger "Under Construction"
This documentation is a work in progress. Some recipes, multiblocks, or materials may be missing or subject to change.

If you find an issue or have a suggestion, come chat with us on [our Discord](https://discord.gg/KBNst7hZ4C).
