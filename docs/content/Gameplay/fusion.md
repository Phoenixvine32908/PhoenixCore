The Phoenixware Fusion Mk1 operates similarly to standard fusion reactors but at a much higher tier (**UIV**).

1. **Fueling**: Inject fusion fuels via fluid hatches.
2. **Pre-heating**: Ensure the reactor has sufficient energy to reach the ignition temperature.
